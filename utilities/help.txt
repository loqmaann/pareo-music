-intro        - Bot introduction

-(j)oin - Let the bot join the voice channel

-leave - Let the bot leave the voice channel

-(p)lay [song]  - Play and/or enqueues a song
    [song] can be a youtube link or a name of the song

-(s)earch [song] - Search song via youtube search
[song] is a name of the song

-queue        - Display the list of the song currently in the queue

-skip         - Skip a song in the queue

-stop         - Clear the queue

-remove [digit] - Remove a song in the queue list

-loop none/one/all/status - Change the loop status

-rand [digit] - Generates a random number with given digit (yep, that's it)
